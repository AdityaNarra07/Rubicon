Investment Criteria

We are looking for established, cash-flowing businesses with reputations worth preserving and operations that can benefit from focused ownership.

Industry Focus

  • HVAC and indoor air quality
  • Plumbing and electrical services
  • Residential cleaning and recurring maintenance
  • Landscaping, pest control, and adjacent services

Business Profile

  • Stable revenue with repeat or recurring demand
  • Strong local brand and service reputation
  • Documented operations or team infrastructure
  • Opportunity to improve sales, pricing, or systems

Preferred Transaction Types

  • Owner retirements or succession transitions
  • Majority buyouts with seller support
  • SBA-backed transactions
  • Deals involving seller financing

Our Approach

We are not looking for a quick flip. Our goal is to build a durable operating platform by preserving what already works and improving what can scale.

Respect for Legacy

We understand that many small businesses represent decades of work, reputation, and trust. Seller transition support and continuity matter.

Operational Improvement

We focus on practical levers such as CRM adoption, lead management, pricing discipline, customer retention, and service consistency.

Long-Term Ownership

We aim to grow through disciplined execution, thoughtful hiring, and reinvestment—not financial engineering alone.
